Jon Mitchell

Jon Mitchell is a Staff Writer for ReadWrite, joining the team in July, 2011. He's based in San Francisco. Before ReadWrite, he was managing editor of NewsTrust, which provides Web-based tools to help consumers, students, and journalists separate fact from fiction in the news. Jon covers Apple, Google, and how tech affects people.
